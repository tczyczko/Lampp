Vector Spaces

In order to continue our exploration of applications, we must first return to more abstract reasoning. Readers should consider these next four chapters as a break from real-world problems, a vacation where we are engaging in a game with symbols and well-defined rules. Later on we will see how the rules of the game and our playing skills can have tremendous benefit in arriving at solutions to concrete problems.
